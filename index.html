Whispering Pine Loop

Nestled in the heart of an ancient pine forest, the Whispering Pine Loop offers hikers a serene 5-mile journey through towering trees whose branches seem to whisper tales of old. The trail is gentle, making it perfect for families and those seeking a peaceful escape into nature. Along the way, you'll find clearings that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, making it a photographer's dream.

Crystal Lake Trail

Crystal Lake Trail is a moderate 3-mile loop that winds through lush meadows before arriving at the sparkling waters of Crystal Lake. The trail is well-known for its wildflowers in spring and early summer, creating a vibrant tapestry of colors that dazzle the eye. The lake itself is a serene spot for picnics, photography, or simply soaking in the tranquil beauty of nature.

Eagle's Crest Path

This challenging 8-mile trail ascends sharply to the rugged peaks of Eagle's Crest. It's a journey meant for the adventurous soul, offering steep inclines, rocky paths, and the ultimate reward: an unparalleled panoramic view from the top. Suitable for experienced hikers, the trail also features several spots to observe eagles in flight, adding a thrilling element to this wild adventure.
